---
title: Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT)
description: Build psychological flexibility by accepting inner experience and taking values‑guided action.
date: 2025-01-21
tags: [act]
---

ACT helps you notice difficult thoughts and feelings without getting tangled in them, and move toward what matters. Core processes include acceptance, defusion, present‑moment attention, self‑as‑context, values, and committed action.
